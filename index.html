CITIZENS TO ELECT JEFF THURMOND

Experience

"I AM NOT A POLITICIAN, and I won't pretend to be one", says Jeff Thurmund. He is the Senior Franklin Co. Highway Dept. Supervisor of District 2 and has 30 years of experience with the Franklin County Highway Department.

 

Community

Jeff is currently serving as a board member of the Franklin County FFA Foundation, and on the Sullivan FFA Advisory Board. He is a member of the Meramec Area Sportsman's Association of Sullivan, and of the Fraternal Order Of Eagles. He has also served as the President of the Sullivan Lions Club for four years.

 

Family

Jeff is a life long resident of Stanton Missouri and graduated from Sullivan High School. He is currently residing in Stanton where he and his wife Peggy live. They raise a few head of cattle. He has one son, and two daughters who have blessed him with four beautiful grandchildren.
